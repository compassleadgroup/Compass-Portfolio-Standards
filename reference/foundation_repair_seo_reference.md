| Blog posts | 25–40 | 40–60 |
| GBP reviews | 40+ @ 4.7★+ | 75+ @ 4.7★+ |
| GBP photos | 75+ | 150+ |
| Time to page 1 | 6–9 mo | 10–14 mo |
| Time to top 3 | 10–14 mo | 16–22 mo |
| Time to Map Pack top 3 | 12–18 mo | 18–30 mo |

**Required schema (all markets):** LocalBusiness, Organization, Service, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList, AggregateRating, Review. Bonus (rare among incumbents): Person, HowTo, VideoObject.

**Referring domains is more discriminating than DR.** Page 1 locals: 50–150 ref domains. Page 2 locals: <30.

**Link mix on page 1:** 50–65% citations (BBB, Angi, HomeAdvisor, Yelp, Yellow Pages, Nextdoor, Manta, MapQuest, Chamber), 15–25% editorial/news, 5–10% associations/certs, rest social. Anchor mix: 60–75% branded, 10–15% naked URL, 10–25% keyword. Over-optimized keyword anchors (>40%) correlate with stuck-on-page-2.

## GBP / Local Pack Patterns

- **40 reviews = minimum credible floor** for Map Pack entry. <20 reviews = excluded unless searcher is within 0.5mi of pin.
- **4.7★ avg** is the Map Pack norm. Sub-4.0★ rarely ranks.
- **Photos:** Page 1 GBPs = 50–200+ (before/after, crew, equipment, neighborhood jobs). Page 2 = <20.
- **Posting cadence:** Most page 1 incumbents post 1–4× per month. Only FRS/Groundworks/Olshan post weekly. **Daily/weekly posting is differentiating in 2026** — under-exploited gap.
- **Service area:** Page 1 sites name 5–15 cities/suburbs. Page 2 typically defaults to single-city.
- **Strong GBP can substitute for weak organic** in the Map Pack (Pier King). Strong organic alone cannot win Map Pack without reviews.

## 4-Phase Sequencing

**Phase 1 (months 0–2):** 60–80 pages live (10–12 services + 15–25 suburbs + 20–30 blog posts). Full schema. GBP verified with 50+ photos pre-launch. 30–40 citations.

**Phase 2 (months 2–5):** 30–50 ref domains via local press, chamber sponsorships, Little League/HS team sponsorships ($500–1,500 = .org link), HARO/Connectively, BBB Torch nomination. 5–10 new Google reviews/month.

**Phase 3 (months 5–9):** +30–50 pages targeting longtail gaps. Case studies with real photos. 75–150 ref domains. GBP at 40–75 reviews + weekly posts + 100+ photos.

**Phase 4 (months 9–18):** Defend, expand adjacent suburbs, add HowTo/VideoObject schema, realtor Lunch & Learns. DR 25+, 150+ ref doms, 100+ reviews.

## Caveats

- DR/ref-domain figures are directional. For exact numbers: 1-month Ahrefs Lite (~$129) or Moz Pro trial, bulk-check all 25+ domains.
- **Map Pack is proximity-weighted.** Multiple physical addresses materially expand reach, but Google has been aggressive removing virtual-office GBPs since 2024.
- Olshan's actual DR may be lower than estimated (90-year link decay). Groundworks may be higher given KKR-backed press density.
- **Editorial "Best of" links carry outsized weight.** Foundation Pro's 8 consecutive Arkansas Democrat-Gazette Reader's Poll wins likely outweigh 50 directory citations. Local awards strategy = year-1 priority.
- FRS corporate HQ is Moberly, MO (not Lee's Summit, which is a regional office). Origin: Sedalia, MO 1992.
