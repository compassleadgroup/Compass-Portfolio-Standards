# Local SEO 2026 — SAB Reference (Rank-and-Rent, No Office in Target City)

## Hard Rules

- **Registered agent address = 0% GBP viability.** Won't verify; if it does, ranks at the agent's location, not target city. [Boost Suite]
- **UPS Store / PO Box / CMRA = explicit GBP guideline violation.** Won't pass video verification.
- **Virtual office / coworking = 5–15% half-life.** Unstaffed = violation. 42% of suspensions involve address verification failures (Sterling Sky 2024).
- **Service area polygon has zero ranking impact.** Verified address centroid (1–5 mile radius) defines where you rank, regardless of areas listed. [Whitespark / Darren Shaw]
- **Video verification is now default for SABs.** Must show: street sign at verified address, branded vehicle, tools, business document, unedited, ≥30 sec. Postcard verification effectively dead.
- **Hiding address has NEGATIVE correlation with "near me" rankings.** Pure SABs structurally underperform hybrid/storefront. [Sterling Sky, n=8,186 businesses, Nov 5, 2025]

## Critical 2026 Findings

- **Review recency > review volume.** "Reviews this month matter more than total." [Sterling Sky, Nov 5, 2025] Stop reviewing for 18 days → "rankings fell off a cliff."
- **AI Overviews appear on 68% of local queries** (Whitespark Q1 2025). 92–97% for hybrid-intent queries ("average cost of X in [city]"). +58% YoY coverage Feb 2025–Feb 2026 (Ahrefs).
- **AI Overviews drop organic CTR 61%, paid CTR 68%** [Seer Interactive, Nov 4, 2025, n=3,119 queries, 25.1M impressions]. BUT: sites cited *within* AIOs see +35% organic CTR, +91% paid CTR.
- **Local packs appear on only 39% of local queries.** AI Overviews now dominate.
- **45% of consumers use generative AI for local recommendations** (up from 6% in 2025). ChatGPT now 3rd most popular local recommendation source after Google + Facebook. [BrightLocal 2026 LCRS, n=1,002]
- **Apple Maps usage doubled**: 14% → 27% of consumers (BrightLocal 2026).
- **Star threshold inflation**: 31% of consumers will *only* use 4.5+ star businesses (up from 17%). 68% require 4+ stars (up from 55%).
- **FTC Consumer Review Rule** (effective Oct 2024): Civil penalties up to **$53,088 per violation** for fake reviews / review gating.
- **GBP edits via Gemini moderation** = real-time flagging of keyword-stuffed names, suspicious category changes.

## What Actually Works (Legitimate Tactics)

- **Service cluster on-page architecture** (NOT a flat sitemap): hub + sub-services + city/neighborhood pages, each with unique local content. Generic city pages got crushed Dec 2025 update.
- **Schema**: LocalBusiness + Service + `areaServed` + `serviceType` + `priceRange` + `aggregateRating`. Use Organization schema (not LocalBusiness) if pure organic, no physical location.
- **Third-party reviews** as substitute for GBP reviews: BBB, Angi, HomeAdvisor, Houzz, Yelp, Facebook. Text content matters more than count/rating.
- **Unstructured citations** > structured. Top citation factor (AI Search): "Presence of business on expert-curated 'best of' and similar lists." Target: local press, Tulsa World, chambers, trade associations, HOA newsletters.
- **Real before/after photos** — GBP weights heavier in 2026.
- **Steady review velocity** matching/beating local competitors. Match pace OR LOSE.
- **Local link building**: chambers, trade associations, sponsorships, local press.
- **Homepage as conversion asset** — +10.7% homepage traffic from LLM citation behavior (Whitespark Q2 2025).
- **LSAs via partner contractor** — placement sits ABOVE local pack. Requires Google background check (not available to lead-gen directly).

## Legal Posture

- Asayag complaint cited "lead-generation schemes" as fraudulent.
- **Disclosure language is now best practice**: "[Brand] is a referral service connecting homeowners with licensed local contractors." Reduces legal risk substantially.
- Operate via LLC on back end; brand customer-facing site locally; never name LLC or RA address on GBP.

## Niche-Specific Notes (Foundation Repair)

- Not on Google's explicit "duress vertical" list (locksmiths, towing, HVAC, plumbing, roadside) — but shares all spam-pattern characteristics (high-ticket emergency, panicked homeowner searches, lead-gen-heavy SERP).
- Algorithmic scrutiny likely similar to plumbing/HVAC.
- CPC $25–$100+. Average ticket $5K–$25K. One closed job/mo = profitable.
- Hybrid-intent queries ("foundation repair cost Tulsa") trigger AIOs 92–97% — prioritize informational content for AIO citation.

## Thresholds That Change Strategy

- Google reverses SAB hiding penalty → reconsider pure-SAB GBP.
- Local contractor partner available → shift to partnership immediately (2–3x ceiling).
- Future core update rewards disclosure → add explicit referral-service language.
- Partner GBP suspended → contingency contractor must be pre-identified.
