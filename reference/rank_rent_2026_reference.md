# Rank-and-Rent 2026 Reference (Organic-Only, No GBP)

## Timelines & Budgets

| Competition | Time to Page 1 | Total Spend | Typical Rent |
|---|---|---|---|
| Low (75K–250K pop, comps <50 RDs) | 4–7 mo typical, 9–12 worst | $1.5K–$4K | $500–$1.5K/mo |
| Medium (250K–1M, comps 50–300 RDs) | 9–14 mo typical, 18–24 worst | $8K–$20K | $1K–$2.5K/mo |
| High (PI law, plumbing LA, etc.) | 18–30 mo, often never | $50K–$150K | Usually bad ROI solo |

Tulsa foundation repair = **medium**, not low. Olshan, Edens, Ram Jack OK entrenched. Plan 9–14 months.

## Build Playbook (Months 0–12)

**Month 0**
- Partial-match brandable domain. EMDs now trigger suspicion. Aged domains risky post-Mar 2024 expired-domain-abuse policy unless topically aligned.
- WordPress + Rank Math + Cloudflare. CWV: LCP <2.5s, INP <200ms (replaced FID Mar 2024), CLS <0.1.
- "Fake business" solve: founder bio + photo, author bios with credentials, dedicated phone (CallRail/Twilio), real mailing address (not virtual office), consistent NAP.

**Months 1–2 — On-Page**
- Homepage (service + city) → 5–10 service hubs (1.5K–2.5K words) → 8–15 neighborhood pages (NOT 300 — Sterling Sky documented manual penalties even with reworded content) → 30–60 informational posts over 12 mo.
- Schema (JSON-LD): LocalBusiness, Service, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList, Article, Organization.
- Hub-and-spoke internal linking, varied anchors.
- AI-drafted + human-edited works. Raw AI does not. Cadence 4–8 posts/mo, not 30 in week one.

**Months 2–4**
- Top 25 citations only, manual, consistent NAP. ROI flattens after 25.
- Link velocity 1–3/mo: HARO/Featured pitches, $100–$500 local sponsorships, $150–$400 local guest posts.

**Months 4–8**
- Link velocity 3–6/mo.
- Digital PR: 1–2 data studies/yr ($2K–$5K), pitch local news + home-improvement pubs.
- Niche edits $141–$400, only on Ahrefs/Semrush-verified traffic sites.
- Podcast guesting (free, builds brand search).
- Parasite layer: YouTube 2–4 vids/mo, Reddit (aged accounts, no spam), LinkedIn, Medium.

**Months 8–12 — Monetize**
- Target by mo 8: 60–100 indexed pages, 20–50 RDs, page-1 for 5–20 long-tail, 5–25 calls/mo.
- Renter close: route 5–10 free leads to one contractor first, prove with call recordings, close $500–$3K/mo flat or $30–$150/lead.

## What's New in 2026

- **Brand search volume = near-mandatory ranking signal** (Matt Diggity 2025 study, 1,000+ sites).
- **Branded mentions correlate r=0.664 with AIO appearance** vs. backlinks r=0.218 (Ahrefs Dec 2025).
- **AIOs hit 49.92% of queries Q4 2025** (Advanced Web Ranking), up from 18.55% Q3 2024. Cited brands +35% clicks. AIO visitors 4.4× more likely to convert (Semrush Jun 2025, 500+ B2B topics).
- **76.1% of AIO-cited URLs also rank top-10** (Ahrefs Oct 2025) — traditional SEO still the foundation.
- **AIO CTR impact:** organic CTR on AIO queries 1.76% → 0.61%, 65% relative decline (Seer Interactive Sept 2025, 25.1M impressions).
- **Reddit went from 20th to 2nd in mobile SERP SoV** (Trisolute mid-2025) after Feb 2024 Google $60M/yr deal. Reddit = 37% of top-10 results, 44% of AIO social-media citations (Profound Jan 2026).
- **Most-cited ChatGPT page types:** Wikipedia 29.7%, homepage/landing 23.8%, educational 19.4% (Ahrefs Oct 2025).
- **13.08% of top-ranked Google content is AI-generated**, up from 2.3% pre-GPT-2 (Influencer Marketing Hub).
- **Traffic diversity** (non-Google share) now a signal per Yandex leak. Aim <80% organic share.

## What's Dead

- 300-city template swaps. Sterling Sky penalty cases confirm rewording isn't enough.
- Raw unedited AI content (Mar 2024 scaled-content-abuse policy captures it regardless of method).
- Mass directory packages ("500 citations $99").
- PBNs at money sites. Works ~3 mo then catastrophic. Tier-2 only if at all.
- EMDs as standalone strategy.
- Exact-match anchor manipulation — now negative signal.
- Comment/forum/profile spam.
- Parasite SEO on Forbes/CNN/big publishers — killed by May 2024 manual + Aug 2025 algo site-reputation-abuse waves.
- Cision HARO — permanently shut Dec 9, 2024. Connectively also dead.
- Off-topic expired domains.
- Programmatic SEO without unique per-page data.
- Authority Hacker discontinued TASS/AH Pro late 2024 citing helpful content update — structural signal easy-mode is over.

## Decision Thresholds

- **Page 1 in <90 days:** Market too easy. Competitor enters in 6–12 mo. Stack defensive links/content while monetizing.
- **Core update drops 40%+:** Pause link building. Audit content depth. Don't panic-disavow.
- **Month 9, <5 leads/mo:** Conversion problem, not ranking. Fix headlines/CTAs/social proof/form friction before more SEO spend.
- **Well-funded competitor enters:** Pivot to long-tail neighborhood × service combos. Don't defend head terms.

## Caveats

- Google ran 3 confirmed core updates in 2025 (Mar 13–27, Jun 30–Jul 17, Dec 11–29) + spam updates. Expect 2+ majors/yr.
- Helpful content folded into core ranking Mar 2024 — site-wide quality continuous. One bad batch of thin pages tanks whole domain.
- Build sequentially. Every documented successful operator built one at a time.
- Lead values: foundation $30–$150/lead, PI law $200–$2K/lead, plumbing/HVAC $20–$80/lead. Rent = local PPC CPC × conversion rate × 30–50%.
- Fictitious-business legal exposure varies (CA, FL, NY stricter). Lawyer up beyond 3–4 sites.
