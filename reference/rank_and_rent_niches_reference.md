# Rank-and-Rent Home Service Niches — 2026 Reference

**Operator context:** Compass Camper LLC (Oregon). Existing site: Tulsa Foundation Experts. US nationwide, rank-and-rent model.

## Selection Criteria
1. Avg ticket $3,000+ (ideally $5,000+) — below that, rent ceiling caps ~$800/mo
2. Emergency/distress intent > discretionary intent
3. Aging housing stock > raw population
4. Insurance-funded jobs = higher conversion + bigger tickets
5. No national consolidator dominating local search
6. Regulatory licensing = moat (fewer competing sites)
7. 1990s-era regional independents with bad websites = ideal targets

## Avoid (Look Attractive But Aren't)
- **Hardscaping / retaining walls / patios** — no per-lead buying, 4–8 week bid cycle, portfolio-driven
- **Locksmiths** — Google restricted GBP verification for non-physical locksmiths
- **Towing** — low ticket, commodity, bad-actor reputation
- **Pool resurfacing** — Angi/Thumbtack saturated, highly seasonal
- **Junk removal** — $250–500 ticket; College Hunks + 1-800-Got-Junk own brand search
- **Epoxy garage floors** — $3K cap; Penntek/GarageExperts have franchise scale
- **Window replacement (whole house)** — Renewal by Andersen + Pella dominate paid + organic
- **General handyman** — sub-$500 avg ticket
- **Heat pump retrofit** — IRA 25C credit terminated July 2025; urgency catalyst removed

## Emerging Niches (Watch / Early Entry)
1. **EV charger installation (residential L2 + panel upgrades)** — $1,200–5,000+. Federal EV credit expired Sept 30, 2025; market softened to 6.5% share (Feb 2026) from 8.1% peak. Few R&R sites exist. Metros: CA, WA, OR, CO, NY, MA, NJ, FL.
2. **Battery backup / whole-home generator** — Powerwall $10K–20K, Generac $4K–15K. Grid-reliability driven (TX, CA, FL). Generator-dealer dominance is thin.
3. **PFAS / water treatment / whole-home filtration** — $3K–10K. Contamination-affected metros: Wilmington NC, Parkersburg WV, Cape Cod, parts of MI, New England.
4. **Aging-in-place mods (stair lifts, walk-in tubs, ramps)** — $3K–30K. Demographics-driven; few dedicated R&R sites.
5. **Fire-hardening / wildfire defensible space (West Coast)** — $2K–15K. New CA regs (AB 3074) driving mandatory work. Almost zero R&R presence. CA WUI, OR, WA fire zones.
6. **Garage gym / home gym buildouts** — $5K–50K high-end. Thin competition.

## Launch Sequence

**Stage 1 (0–60 days):** Sewer line / trenchless in secondary metro with old infrastructure. Targets: KC, Memphis, OKC, Springfield MO, Wichita. Reuse Tulsa Foundation Experts architecture. Goal: $1,500–2,500/mo within 6 months.

**Stage 2 (90–120 days):** Crawl space encapsulation in Charlotte, Nashville, Raleigh, or Atlanta. Avoid Groundworks-dominated metros. Goal: $1,000–2,000/mo within 6 months.

**Stage 3 (6–9 mo):** Pick ONE based on metro fit:
- Mold remediation in humid secondary metro (Memphis, Mobile, Jax)
- Hoarder/biohazard in any 500K+ metro
- Septic install/pumping in rural-adjacent OK metros

**Stage 4 (12+ mo):** One emerging niche — EV charger in top adoption metro (Bay, Seattle, Denver, Boston, Austin) or fire-hardening in CA.

**Kill criteria:**
- Stage 1 site doesn't crack top 5 Maps in 4 months → pivot budget to emerging niche
- Groundworks/PE rollup acquires major operator in your target metro → pivot metro immediately
- Any metro with no AAAC Wildlife subdomain on page 1 → green-light wildlife as fast side niche
- Exclusive water damage call leads drop below $200 in target metro → niche is commoditized, skip

## Caveats
- Per-lead/CPC numbers from lead-gen marketing pages (33 Mile Radius, Inquirly, Service Direct, ResultCalls, Hoom, Downey) = upper-bound willingness-to-pay, not guaranteed rates. Validate with Keyword Planner per metro.
- The $500/click water damage figure is from 2012 — illustrative, not current.
- DR-evidence of weak page-1 competition not surfaced in published case studies. Run Ahrefs/SEMrush per candidate metro before launching.
- Policy environment shifted mid-2025 (OBBBA P.L. 119-21 terminated IRA 25C; EV credit expired 9/30/25). Re-check annually.
- "Average ticket" hides huge variance — lead-quality filtering > raw volume.
- PE consolidation (Groundworks-type) is biggest disruption risk in foundation/waterproofing/crawl space. Diversify across buyer pools as hedge.
